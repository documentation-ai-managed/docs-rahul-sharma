## Getting Started

1. Sign in at `https://docs.rahulsharma.com`.
2. Click "New Project" on your dashboard.
3. Enter a project name and description.
4. Add team members by email (optional).
5. Start creating pages for your documentation.

## Quick Tip

You can organize docs with tags and folders for easy access. Save your changes regularly!
