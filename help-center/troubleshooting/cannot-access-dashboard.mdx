description: Fix common login and access issues with Rahul Sharma's dashboard.
---

## Symptoms

- Stuck on login screen
- Dashboard not loading

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    Make sure you are online and try reloading the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Try clearing your cache/cookies and login again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset Password">
    If you forgot your password, click "Forgot Password?" on the login page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
