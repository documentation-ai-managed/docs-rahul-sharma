## Overview

Keep your documentation tidy and accessible.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new folder">
    Go to your project, click "New Folder", and name it (e.g. API Docs).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move pages into folders">
    Drag and drop existing pages into your new folder for better structure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use tags for quick search">
    Add tags to pages so you can filter and find them fast.
  </Step>
</Steps>
